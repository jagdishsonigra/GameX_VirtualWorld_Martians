# 🌌 Shadow of Me (VR)

> *Unveil What Lies Beyond*

---

## 🎮 Overview

**Shadow of Me (VR)** is a **psychological horror puzzle game** built exclusively for Virtual Reality.

Developed during **Game X Innovate Hacksprint 7.0 at MMCOE**, the game challenges players to question reality itself — where **perception becomes the puzzle**.

Unlike traditional horror games relying on jump scares, this experience focuses on **immersion, illusion, and decision-making under pressure**. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

---

## 🧠 Core Idea

> *What if reality itself becomes the puzzle?*

Most VR horror games rely on linear puzzles and jump scares.  
**Shadow of Me** introduces:

- 🌀 Dual-reality gameplay
- 🧩 Perception-based puzzles
- 🎭 Illusion vs Reality mechanics

VR is not just the platform — **it is the core mechanic**. 

---

## 🔁 Core Gameplay Loop

1. 🔍 Explore the environment  
2. 📜 Discover hidden clues  
3. 🎭 Switch between realities using a wearable mask  
4. 🧠 Interpret illusion vs reality  
5. 🔓 Solve puzzles and progress  

Players navigate a **shifting labyrinth** and uncover hidden truths through interaction and observation.

---

## 🎯 Objective

- Navigate **4 progressively challenging chambers**
- Identify the **correct treasure chest**
- Solve puzzles before **time runs out**

✔️ Win: Open the correct final chest  
❌ Lose: Time expires 

---

## 🕹️ Game Mechanics

### 🎚️ Progression
- 4 Levels → Increasing difficulty  
- Perception-based puzzles  
- Failure reveals hidden clues (not punishment)

### 🎮 Controls
- Grab & rotate objects  
- Place puzzle elements  
- Wear / remove mask (reality switch)  
- Free movement in VR space

- Real-time **world switching system**
- **State-driven architecture** with layered rendering
- **Modular puzzle system** (FSM + event-driven)
- Optimized for **120+ FPS VR performance** using:
  - Static batching  
  - Baked lighting  
  - Occlusion culling
