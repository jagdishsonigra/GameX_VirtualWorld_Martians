# 🌌 Shadow of Me (VR)

> *“Perception is not reality… but it decides your fate.”*

---

## 🎮 Overview

**Shadow of Me (VR)** is a **psychological horror puzzle experience** built exclusively for Virtual Reality.

Developed during **Game X Innovate Hacksprint 7.0 at MMCOE**, the game immerses players in a mysterious mansion where **reality shifts, perception deceives, and every decision matters**.

Instead of relying on jump scares, the game creates tension through **uncertainty, illusion, and psychological pressure**.

---

## 🧠 Core Concept

> *What if what you see… is not what is real?*

At the heart of the experience lies a unique mechanic:

🎭 **The Mask of Perception**

- Reveals a hidden version of the world  
- Highlights patterns, dangers, and clues  
- But… **does not always tell the truth**

Players must constantly question:

> “Am I seeing reality… or just a version of it?”

---

## 🔁 Core Gameplay Loop

1. 🔍 Explore the mansion  
2. 📜 Discover environmental clues  
3. 🎭 Wear / remove the mask to shift perception  
4. 🧠 Interpret illusion vs reality  
5. 🔓 Solve puzzles and progress deeper  

---

## 🏚️ Story Flow

- 🏠 A hidden **treasure beneath an abandoned mansion**
- 🗿 Rotating statues unlock the first mechanism  
- 🎭 A mysterious **mask** introduces dual reality  
- 🧩 Symbols and illusions guide (and misguide) you  
- ⚠️ A warning appears: **“Trust only in reality”**  
- 🧠 Reality begins to contradict perception  
- 🔻 Falling is not failure — it reveals the truth  
- 🎯 Final test: Choose the correct chest using observation  

---

## 🎯 Objective

- Navigate **4 perception-driven levels**
- Solve increasingly complex puzzles  
- Identify the **correct final chest**

✔️ **Win:** Choose the correct chest  
❌ **Lose:** Time runs out or wrong decisions  

---

## 🕹️ Key Mechanics

### 🎭 Dual Reality System
- Switch between **real world** and **perception world**
- Each reveals different information

### 🧠 Perception-Based Puzzles
- Not all visual cues are trustworthy  
- Players must **think beyond what they see**

### 🔄 Failure as Progress
- Falling or making mistakes unlocks **new paths**
- Encourages experimentation instead of punishment

### 🎧 Environmental Storytelling
- Audio cues + visual hints instead of UI  
- Immersion-first design philosophy
